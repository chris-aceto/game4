Reluctant Cat-pack Kerfuffle

  • CONTROLS Arrow keys move left and right. Press space to fly. Press the up arrow key to boost. Down arrow key to Super Boost
  • High Concept: Flight Control a jet-pack to ascend, using boost and super boost as alternative speeds with appropriate cooldown.
  • Player Experience Goals: Moderate There's no real way to lose, simply fun for those who like to go fast.
  • Genre: Flight Sim The mechanics are slightly ambiguous here. Raising and lower altitude while dodging sports cars.
  • Game play: The player controls a cat with no choice but to use a jetpack to traverse through his bizarre dream.
  • Prototype goal: Vertical movement with potential for dodging airborne objects.
  • Features: Reflex training and obstacle course navigation.
  • Setting/Premise: The player controls a cat piloting a jetpack who's only desire is to flee this bizarre dream.
  • Story: With your owners new job, he's been supporting your recovery from underground boxing. The traumatic experiences of your career have left you with confusing dreams, which you must overcome.
  • Target Audience: People who appreciate fast.
  • Play time: Depends on how fast you are. Reasonably about a minute.
  • Strategies: Avoid spamming boost, get in a proper position with nothing above you before boosting.
  • Prototype Assets: Phaser documents online.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Carpool kerfuffle
